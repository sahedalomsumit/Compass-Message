Welcome To Lezara

Lezara Laser & Vein Care is located in Squamish BC. We focus on a clinical approach to non-invasive laser treatments, specifically in the areas of hair removal, vein removal, pigmentation and scar reduction, skin tightening and other laser-based treatments.

Lezara is an independent facility that operates out of the Diamond Head Medical Clinic in the heart of Squamish. We take great care to give you the personal treatment and attention to detail you deserve. No obligation consultations are important to us – we want to make sure our treatments are right for you.
We acknowledge that we live, work, and play on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded territories of xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam), sḵwx̱wú7mesh (Squamish), sel̓íl̓witulh (Tsleil Waututh), and Tla-o-qui-aht (Cloyahuat Sound) First Nations.
